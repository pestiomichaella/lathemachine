## Overview

Parting, also known as cutting off, is the process of cutting a piece off a workpiece while it is rotating on a lathe. This operation uses a narrow, blade-like cutting tool positioned perpendicular to the workpiece.

## Applications

- Trim ends of workpieces
- Cut finished parts from longer stock
- Separate multiple parts from a single bar
- Create distinct sections for further processing

## Step-by-Step Procedure

1. Secure workpiece
2. Install parting tool at center height
3. Turn on lathe and set speed
4. Position tool perpendicular at cut location
5. Feed tool to cut through workpiece
6. Stop lathe and remove parted section

## Safety and Precautions

- Feed the tool slowly and steadily
- Avoid sudden pressure spikes
- Be aware of the cut-off piece becoming free
- Ensure adequate clearance for the falling part
- Never reach toward the parting tool
